General Diet Tips

Simple practices for a healthier you

🥗 Eat More Whole Foods

Include vegetables, fruits, whole grains, and legumes in your daily diet.

🚰 Stay Hydrated

Drink at least 8 glasses of water per day to keep your body functioning well.

🍽️ Portion Control

Avoid overeating by being mindful of your portion sizes and eating slowly.

🧂 Limit Processed Foods

Cut back on sugary drinks, fast food, and packaged snacks where possible.

🕗 Regular Meal Timing

Maintain consistent eating times to support metabolism and digestion.

💤 Sleep & Nutrition

Get enough sleep daily—poor sleep impacts hunger and food choices.
